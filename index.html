About me

Hey there! I’m Rakesh Reddy B, a passionate and versatile tech enthusiast with a solid foundation in software development, web development, game design, and cybersecurity. While I’ve dabbled in a variety of technologies—from C and Java to SQL and PHP—Python is where I truly shine. It’s not just a language to me; it’s a craft I’ve mastered.

My journey spans across developing web applications, exploring the world of pentesting and ethical hacking, and most recently, venturing into the immersive realm of game design. I'm currently working solo on a long-term passion project: a Mahabharata-inspired PlayStation game, handling everything from concept to creation.

“Keep your essence as a secret”—a personal mantra that reminds me to let my work speak louder than my words. Inspired by the brilliant David J. Malan, I aim to bring depth and clarity to every project I touch.

Beyond the code, I’m a coffee lover, a gamer at heart, a hacker by passion, and a true foodie. Known for my positive attitude and charming approach to problem-solving, I thrive in leadership roles, combining focus, dedication, and a drive for excellence in everything I do.
